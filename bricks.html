Brick Buying Guide

Burnt Clay vs Fly Ash?

Burnt clay bricks are traditional and widely used. Fly ash bricks are stronger, eco-friendly and more uniform in shape.

AAC Blocks Benefits

3× lighter than clay bricks, better insulation, faster laying — ideal for high-rise buildings. More expensive but saves labor cost.

First vs Second Class?

First class: uniform shape, hard, no cracks. Use for main walls. Second class: partition walls and non-load bearing areas.

How Many Bricks?

For 1000 sqft house (9-inch wall): approx 55,000 bricks. Use our calculator on homepage for exact estimate.

Standard IS Sizes

190×90×90 mm is IS standard. 230×110×70 mm is modular size. Always verify with engineer for load-bearing walls.

Minimum Order

Minimum 500 bricks. Delivery by tractor or truck. Unloading at site included. Bulk orders get extra discount.

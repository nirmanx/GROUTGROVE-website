How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last Updated: April 2026 | Effective: April 2026

1. Who We Are (GroutGrove)

GroutGrove is an online construction material marketplace operating in Delhi NCR region of India. We connect buyers (customers) with verified sellers of construction materials including cement, steel, bricks, tiles, and more.

📍 Service Area: Delhi, Noida, Greater Noida, Ghaziabad, Gurugram, Faridabad

📧 Email: support@groutgrove.in

2. Information We Collect

From Customers:

  • Name, phone number, email address
  • Delivery address and pincode
  • Order history and cart items
  • City and area for product recommendations

From Sellers:

  • Business name, owner name, WhatsApp number
  • Business address, city, state, pincode
  • Product listings, prices, categories
  • GST number (optional)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and confirm your orders
  • To send WhatsApp order confirmations
  • To show you products available near your pincode
  • To connect buyers with nearby verified sellers
  • To improve our platform and services
  • To send important updates about your orders
🔒 We do NOT sell your personal data to any third party. Ever.

4. Customer Data Protection

  • Your personal phone number is NEVER shared with sellers directly
  • All orders are routed through GroutGrove team
  • Sellers only receive order details, not your full profile
  • Payment information is processed securely
  • Your account password is stored securely

5. Seller Data Protection

  • Seller WhatsApp number is not publicly displayed on website
  • Orders are forwarded through GroutGrove admin
  • Business information is used only for marketplace operations
  • Sellers can request data deletion by contacting us

6. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (a secure cloud database platform). Data is stored in India-compliant cloud servers. We follow industry-standard security practices.

7. Cookies & Browser Storage

We use browser localStorage to:

  • Remember your cart items between sessions
  • Keep you logged in
  • Save your delivery preferences

You can clear this data by clearing your browser history at any time.

8. Your Rights

  • Access: You can request a copy of your data
  • Correction: You can update your information
  • Deletion: You can request account deletion
  • Opt-out: You can opt out of marketing messages
